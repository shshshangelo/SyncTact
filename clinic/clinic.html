LOGO

The logo for SyncTact is a powerful representation of the clinic's mission to help patients overcome neurological challenges and transform their lives. The S in SyncTact is designed to resemble the spinal vertebrae, which serves as the protective barrier of the spinal cord, whereas the wings symbolize hope, healing, and transformation. The circle around the logo represents unity, wholeness, and overall holistic practice, which is relevant to the cyclical rehabilitation process. Rather than using the original caduceus, the rod was replaced with the spinal cord, emphasizing the clinic's focus on neurorehabilitation. The spinal cord addition creates a more specific and direct representation of the clinic's commitment to treating spinal cord injuries and other neurological conditions. The logo embodies the hope and potential for progress in neurorehabilitation and underscores the supportive and comprehensive care provided by SyncTact to patients on their journeys toward healing and recovery.
